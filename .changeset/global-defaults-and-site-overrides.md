---

Set a default shadow and opacity that apply to every site, and override per-site only where you want something different. `www.example.com` and `example.com` are treated as the same site; real subdomains (like `app.example.com`) are separate, so an app under a subdomain isn't affected by settings on the marketing site. A site override can also force the overlay off on a single site while leaving it enabled everywhere else.
